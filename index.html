About Us

Come Hangout with us…

If you want to catch up with friends over excellent barista coffee, have an intimate meal for two , meet up with the boys ( or girls) for wicked cocktails, or even enjoy a weekend breakfast, hangout on 20 Preston will provide the perfect backdrop.
Tantalize your taste buds with our tapas and soothe your soul with our soundtrack of jazz and smooth chill music in addition to supporting local talent who take centre stage on the weekend evenings. Don’t worry if you find it hard to decide what to order when faced with the mouth-watering choices on our menu, Our friendly staff will be happy to help make that “all so difficult ” meal or drink choice that much easier with their fine knowledge of the specials of the day and complementary wine selections.
